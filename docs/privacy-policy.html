Privacy Policy — ND Player

Last updated: March 29, 2026

ND Player ("the app") is a personal music player that connects to a Navidrome server you own or have been granted access to. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data We Collect

ND Player does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party.

  • Server credentials (URL, username, password) are stored exclusively on your device using the operating system's secure credential storage (Android Keystore). They are never sent to anyone other than the Navidrome server you configured.
  • Music and metadata are streamed directly between your device and your Navidrome server. No audio data passes through any developer-controlled infrastructure.
  • Downloaded files are saved locally on your device for offline playback and are not shared with anyone.

2. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or other third-party SDKs that collect user data.

3. Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely to communicate with the Navidrome server address you provide. No other network connections are made.

4. Children's Privacy

ND Player is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

6. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on the project repository or contact the developer directly.
